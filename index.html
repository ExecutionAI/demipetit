About me

Baker, photographer, sweet tooth.

I'm Demi — the photographer, recipe developer, and creative baker behind Petit Demi, based in Amsterdam. What started as a hobby quickly turned into something I couldn't stop.

Every cake is baked to order, decorated with care, and made with the best ingredients I can find. No mass production, no shortcuts.

Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or a Tuesday — if you have a reason to celebrate, I'd love to bake for you.
